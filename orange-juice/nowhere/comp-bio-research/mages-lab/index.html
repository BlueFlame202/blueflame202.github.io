Thoughts on use of optimal transport:

I thought the use of partially balanced optimal transport was cool. In particular, some interesting points I realized are:

  • The discrete case corresponds to a linear programming problem.
  • Here, they use entropy regularized partially balanced optimal transport. The entropy regularization is for tractability, and the partially balanced is a situation where one of the distributions in the optimal transport is not fixed.
In particular, the use of partially balanced OT was pretty clever and cool, and even feels universal in some sense.

General Thoughts:

  • I thought merging different existing techniques into a TACCO framework was pretty cool.

However, I feel that maybe UMAP distances are probably reliable in most cases except maybe pathological cases, or at least in most cases they should provide signal?
