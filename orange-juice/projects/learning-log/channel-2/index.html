Differential Equations

June 25th, 2024 (New!)

Welcome! Today's broadcast features a general overview of methods to solve PDEs! We summarize methods which were taught in the Fall 2023 rendition of Berkeley's MATH 126, with some additions. For example, we mention the method of characteristics and fourier methods, as well as various inequalities (such as Gronwall's inequality) which are effective for showing energy estimates which help with uniqueness and stability of solutions.
