Fitness

June 26th, 2024

Welcome! Today's broadcast features a plan to investigate fitness! I personally don't (yet) place enough emphasis on fitness in my life; it has always come secondary to other things. This channel is an attempt to change that. Over the next month, we will be monitoring my calorie intake, estimates on macro intakes, and exercise regimen in order to eventually quantitatively model my health.
